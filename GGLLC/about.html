About Us

Green Gemini LLC is a local florist proudly serving Millville, NJ and surrounding areas. Owner of Green Gemini, Katie Carroll, decided to pursue her dream of owning a flower shop in 2018, and has put a fresh, whimsical spin on traditional floral design. Green Gemini specializes in flowers for all occasions, with most creations made-to-order for our customers.

We are dedicated to making your experience at Green Gemini LLC pleasant and seamless.

We also do on-site classes!

Make sure to follow us on Facebook and Instagram for updates and information on classes and upcoming events!
